## Recurring UI Patterns

### Colors
- **Primary**: `#6741d9` (Purple)
- **Primary Light**: `#8a6bd6`
- **Primary BG**: `#f3f0ff` (Very light purple)
- **Accent**: `#ff9f43` (Orange)
- **Text Dark**: `#1f1f2e`
- **Text Subtle**: `#5c5c6d`
- **Background**: `#ffffff`

### Typography
- **Display Font**: Rubik (Hebrew), Manrope (English)
- **Body Font**: Heebo (Hebrew), Manrope (English)

### Components
- **Buttons**: Rounded (1rem), purple primary, white secondary
- **Cards**: Rounded (1-2rem), white background, subtle shadows
- **Icons**: Material Symbols Outlined
- **Layout**: Mobile-first, max-width container, RTL support

### Common Elements
- Header with logo/icon + title
- Back/Close buttons
- Skip buttons (onboarding)
- Progress indicators
- Status badges (green for success, blue for info)
- Blurred background gradients (purple/primary)
